LFD - Large Formate Display

Imperial Techsol LDX series with LED backlights have been specifically designed for use in a wide variety of professional applications. The LCD displays present crisp, clear and color-accurate images and provide an alternative or complementary visualization solution for modern control rooms.

Featuring UHD resolution (3840 x 2160 pixels), the LDX series displays bring the same resolutions as 4 full HD models. Thanks to the LCD technology with LED backlights, this range offers a long lifetime, bright images and a slim design. Available with a screen diagonal of 84" and 58", the LDX series bring great viewing comfort to a multitude of professional environments.
